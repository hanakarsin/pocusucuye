Who is Victoria Rubadiri? Husband, biography, age, salary

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Monday, March 25, 2024

Victoria Rubadiri is a Kenyan news anchor, show host, and media personality. She presents news on Citizen TV, Kenya's most-watched TV station. Victoria and Citizen TV colleague Waihiga Mwaura won a prestigious BBC award in 2020.

Victoria Rubadiri is a household name in East Africa and one of the highest-paid journalists in the region. The lady believes in women's empowerment and second chances. She once told her story on air to inspire teenage mothers.

Victoria Rubadiri's biography

Victoria Rubadiri's mother is a Kalenjin-Masaai, while her father is Malawian. She spent part of her childhood in Kenya and schooled in the US.

The lady grew up in a Christian home, and she associates herself with Christianity to date. Victoria Rubadiri's sister, Mabel Rubadiri, attended Oxford University.

What is Victoria Rubadiri's nationality?

She is a Kenyan citizen by birth. Victoria is yet to reveal if she got American citizenship (after living in the US for many years) and Malawian citizenship (due to her paternity).

How old is Vicky Rubadiri?

Victoria Rubadiri's age is 35 years as of November 2022. She was born in Nairobi, Kenya, in 1987 and moved with her family to the USA at age 10. Rubadiri returned to Kenya with her family in 2010 after completing her university education.

What is Victoria Rubadiri's tribe?

Her ancestry is a mixture of White and African roots. Rubadiri's grandmother has Kikuyu, Maasai, and Seychellois roots, while her grandfather was half Kalenjin and half Scottish.

What did Victoria Rubadiri study?

She joined Atlantic City High School in Atlantic County, New Jersey, in 1999. The lady later earned a Bachelor of Arts in Broadcast Journalism from Temple University in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, in 2009.

Career history

Before graduating, Victoria interned at WMGM – NBC 40 TV as an Assignment Desk Editor in 2007 and was a personal assistant at Angela Crockett Company in Brooklyn, New York, in 2008.

She worked as a presenter and reporter at Capital FM in Nairobi from April 2011 to December 2012. The late Chris Kirubi owned the radio station. Rubadiri later landed a job at NTV. She was a news anchor, reporter, and script editor.

Larry Madowo and Victoria Rubadiri co-anchored NTV's Prime Time Weekend Edition. She also hosted the Victoria's Lounge show on the same TV station. Rubadiri moved to Citizen TV in May 2018 to work as a senior news anchor and won the BBC World News Komla Dumor award in 2020.

Did Victoria Rubadiri quit Citizen TV?

She took a three-month leave from Citizen TV and returned in December 2021. While on leave, Rubadiri travelled to BBC London for work and to learn as part of her 2020 Komla Dumor award. She anchors news alongside co-host Jeff Koinange.

What is Victoria Rubadiri's salary?

Rubadiri is one of the best-paid female news anchors in Kenya. She earned about Ksh 400,000 monthly at NTV. Her salary at Citizen TV is Ksh 750,000 to Ksh 900,000 per month. She also gets Ksh. 100,000 dress allowance.
