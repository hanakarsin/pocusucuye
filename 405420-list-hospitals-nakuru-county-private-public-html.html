List of hospitals in Nakuru county (private and public)

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, July 26, 2024

If you are living in Nakuru County or are visiting the county, you need to know where you can access quality medical services whenever the need arises. The good news is that there are so many private and public hospitals in Nakuru County, which are committed to offering high-quality medical care services at an affordable cost.

Nakuru county has been working tirelessly to improve its healthcare services to the residents. It is one of the counties in Kenya, leading in terms of providing quality and affordable health care services. The county boasts of more than 600 health care facilities mandated to provide efficient, reliable and affordable medical services to residents.

List of hospitals in Nakuru County

When you begin your search for the best hospitals in Nakuru county, you will notice that there are so many hospitals in the county, and you do not need to fumble around hospitals with poor health care services. Here are some of the best facilities you can visit:

1. Mediheal Hospital Nakuru

  • Location: Along Nairobi-Nakuru Highway
  • Postal address: P.O. Box 17226 – 20100
  • Phone number: 0735000735 or 0780 278 000
  • Email address: info.nkr@medihealgroup.com

Mediheal Hospital Nakuru is one of the best private hospitals in Nakuru County. The level 5 private hospital is located along the Nairobi- Nakuru Highway. It is best known for providing high quality and affordable healthcare services. The NHIF accredited facility offers both inpatient and outpatient services.

Its main areas of specialisation include ophthalmology, pathology, dentistry, gynaecology, oncology, dermatology, radiology, cardiology, paediatrics, and so much more. Mediheal is fully equipped with an Intensive Care Unit (ICU), Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU), High Dependency Unit (HDU), maternity ward, operating theatres and a modern casual unit.

2. Valley Hospital

  • Location: West Road, Nakuru
  • Phone number: 051-2214503 or 0723466718
  • Email address: info@valleyhospital-nku.org

Valley Hospital is a premier health facility offering 24-hour outpatient and inpatient services. The services offered at the facility range from diagnostics, ambulatory and inpatient care, emergency and critical care, counselling and rehabilitation services, in addition to specialised services.

Its mission is to provide quality, yet affordable healthcare. Valley Hospital aims to understand the needs of all its patients and families, and it strives to provide the best quality of services.

3. Evans Sunrise Medical Centre

  • Town clinic location: Oyster Shell Building, Kenyatta Avenue
  • Postal address: P.O. Box 15464-20100 Nakuru
  • Phone number: (+254) 51-2214207 or +254 725 911 911
  • Email: info@evans-sunrise-mc.com.

Evans Sunrise Medical Centre is a privately-owned 75-bed hospital located in Nakuru which commenced operations in 1998. It provides a wide range of services, including outpatient, inpatient, emergency medical services, and specialised services.

The clinic strives to provide high quality, cost-effective healthcare services for the community it serves through the promotion of wellness, the alleviation of human suffering and the restoration of health as swiftly, safely, and humanely as possible.

4. Nakuru Specialist Hospital

  • Location: Lanet area next to the DOD
  • Phone number: 0700 907 000
  • Email address: info@nsch.co.ke

The Nakuru Specialist Hospital is an upcoming private hospital in Nakuru County with an inpatient bed capacity of over 60 beds, maternity and operation theatres. It is equipped with modern healthcare facilities.

The hospital offers a variety of specialised services, including major and minor surgery, laparoscopy, ICU, Renal /Dialysis, dental services, Xray, CT Scan, laboratory services, gynaecology, paediatric, well-baby clinics, and so much more.

5. Nakuru War Memorial Hospital

  • Location: State Hse Rd North, 240-20100, Nakuru, Kenya
  • Phone number: 0733636575
  • Email address: nkrwamemorial@gmail.com

This is the oldest and best hospital in Nakuru. The facility boasts of several specialist resident doctors. It offers almost all medical services, including dialysis, accident and emergency service, laboratory, ICU, HDU, pharmacy, ECG, funeral home, antenatal, postnatal, family planning, and so much more.

6. Bliss Medical Centre

  • Location: Apollo Centre, Kenyatta Avenue
  • Phone number: +254 786 412 317
  • Email address: info@blissmedicalcentre.com

Bliss Medical Centre is one of the NHIF accredited hospitals in Nakuru. It is committed to providing exceptional and high-quality healthcare at an affordable cost. The facility provides a wide range of services, including consultation, pharmacy, laboratory, ultrasound, and so much more. Hours of operation are Mon-Sat: 9:00am - 6:00pm.

7. St. Mary's Mission Hospital Elementaita

  • Location: Elementaita along the Gilgil - Nakuru Highway
  • Phone number: 0717 305089
  • Email address: elementaita@stmmh.co.ke

St. Mary's Hospital is headed by the Assumption Sisters of Nairobi and aims to offer quality care at an affordable cost to Nakuru County residents. The hospital has a bed capacity of 150 Patients. Its services are geared towards ensuring that the patients receive quality care that will improve their prognosis.

The facility provides almost all the outpatient and inpatient services, including but not limited to radiology, pharmacy, comprehensive care, MCH and maternity, accident and emergency, laboratory, ophthalmology, and so much more.

8. Nakuru Level 6 Hospital

  • Location: Show Ground Road
  • Phone number: +254 51 2215580

Top on the list of public hospitals in Nakuru county is Nakuru Level 6 Hospital. The hospital was elevated from level 5 into a level 6 facility, making it a national referral hospital in the same rank as Kenyatta National Hospital (KNH) and the Moi Teaching and Referral Hospital.

It, therefore, provides most of the outpatient and inpatient services. The hospital offers 24-hour emergency medical services. Nakuru Level 6 hospital boasts a bed capacity of more than 500 beds.

9. Mercy Mission Hospital Annex

  • Location: Moi Road, Biashara Ward
  • Phone number: 0796560759

It is a leading Christian mission hospital that offers both inpatient and outpatient medical services. The hospital prides itself in providing affordable and high-quality services, which explains why most patients love it.
