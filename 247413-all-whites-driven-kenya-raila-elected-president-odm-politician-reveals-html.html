All Whites will be driven out of Kenya if Raila is elected president - ODM politician reveals

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Friday, August 2, 2024

- Laikipia North Member of Parliament Mathew Lempurkel has claimed that all foreigners living in Laikipia and Kenya will be chased away once Raila Odinga assumes office

- Lempurkel says a white man has already approached him asking him to take his land following the reports

- This is not the first time the controversial MP is making such inciting remarks

A powerful and controversial Orange Democratic Movement (ODM) politician has made some shocking remarks concerning Raila Odinga, foreigners living in Kenya and the August 8 Elections.

Laikipia North Member of Parliament Mathew Lempurkel recently claimed that all white men living in Laikipia County will forcefully be driven out of the area and Kenya if the National Super Alliance(NASA) flagbearer will win the August 8 General Election.

According to Lempurkel, the NASA government would immediately evict all foreigners from Laikipia and Kenya and their land be taken by locals once it gets to power.
