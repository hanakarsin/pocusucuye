Griffin Gluck - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Friday, May 10, 2024

Well known for his Young Artist Award-nominated performance in the feature film Just Go with It, he landed a leading role in the Fox television series Red Band Society as Charlie. He later went on to appear in American Vandal in 2017. He also starred in the film Middle School: The Worst Years of My Life.

Before Fame

He had an early TV appearance in an episode of The Office and played the role of Monty in three episodes of United States of Tara. 

Trivia

He had a recurring role on the series Private Practice that spanned from 2011 to 2013. In 2019, he appeared as Jack Dunkleman in the Netflix movie Tall Girl. 

Family Life

He was born in Los Angeles, California, to Cellin, a director, and Karin, a producer. He has an older sister named Caroline. He began a relationship with Sabrina Carpenter in 2019, but their relationship ended the following year.

Associated With

He had a starring role on the short-lived ABC series Back in the Game opposite James Caan.
