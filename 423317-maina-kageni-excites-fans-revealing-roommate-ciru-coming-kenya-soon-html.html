Maina Kageni Excites Fans after Revealing Roommate Ciru Is Coming to Kenya Soon

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Tuesday, April 30, 2024
  • Maina Kageni has once again disclosed that his roommate Curi will be coming to Kenya in the near future
  • The radio personality and the beautiful lass have become a favourite for many fans online, with their chemistry nearly infectious
  • Maina shared a snap of him and another lady while also reminding his fans to tour the county

Radio host Maina Kageni has delighted many of his followers with news about the homecoming of his roommate Wanjiru wa Kinuthia alias Ciru.

Maina and Ciru have become a favourite duo for many fans who believe she is the right lady for him to marry.

The Classic 105 presenter shared a picture on Instagram of him alongside a beautiful lass, with the two displaying huge smiles.

Maina disclosed in his caption that Ciru will be returning to Kenya soon while reminding fans to tour the county.
