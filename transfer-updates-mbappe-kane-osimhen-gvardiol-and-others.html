Mbappe, Kane, Osimhen, Gvardiol, And Others

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Sunday, May 5, 2024

While PSG excluded Kylian Mbappe from their preseason tour of Asia this summer, transfer talks involving him, Chelsea and the Saudi club Al-Hilal have started, RMC Sport claimed.

PSG’s Mbappe has received the same 400 million euro annual offer from Al-Hilal as Lionel Messi did. The Saudi Pro League club would let him join Real Madrid in the summer of 2024 since Mbappe already has an agreement in principle to join Real Madrid as a free agent next summer, according to Nicholas Schira.

The French champions decided to put him up for sale on Friday, and Mbappe is ready to sit out the full season and play for another team for free next summer, according to Sky Sports.

The wife of Tottenham and England striker Harry Kane, 29, has been spotted in Munich looking at homes and schools before a potential relocation to Bayern Munich, Bild claimed.

Chelsea have gotten in touch with Ajax to express interest in acquiring Mohammed Kudus, a 22-year-old Ghanaian offensive midfielder, according to Sports.

English midfielder Jordan Henderson, 33, has cleared his medical ahead of a £12 million move from Liverpool to a Saudi club, according to Al-Ettifaq.

Jurgen Klopp, the manager of Liverpool, has promised to make additional signings before the season commences to replace Henderson and 29-year-old Brazilian midfielder Fabinho, who are about to join the Saudi Pro League, FourFourTwo claimed.

The 24-year-old Nigerian striker Victor Osimhen’s contract talks with Napoli are still deadlocked, leaving Chelsea or Manchester United free to sign him this summer, according to Goal.

Romelu Lukaku, a 30-year-old Belgian striker for Chelsea, has reportedly spoken to rival clubs AC Milan and Juventus about a possible transfer, leading Inter Milan to believe it is now impossible to re-sign him, Gazzetta dello Sport claimed.

Wilfried Zaha, a 30-year-old forward from the Ivory Coast who is now a free agent after his contract with Crystal Palace expired in June, has been offered an £8 million a year contract by Galatasaray, according to the Mail.

36-year-old Uruguayan striker Luis Suarez is prepared to pay out of his cash to leave Gremio and join Lionel Messi at Inter Miami, according to ESPN Sport.

To sign 21-year-old Croatian defender Josko Gvardiol, Manchester City have been informed that they must match RB Leipzig’s £87 million asking price, according to Saturday Mirror.

Crystal Palace are considering the signature of Aymeric Laporte, a 29-year-old Spain defender who is currently playing for Manchester City, according to Sunday Star.

Despite Palace’s interest in re-signing English right-back Aaron Wan-Bissaka, 25, Manchester United appears prepared to exercise the contract’s additional year option, the Sun claimed.

Michail Antonio, 33, of Jamaica wants to leave West Ham for Steven Gerrard’s Al-Ettifaq this summer, Football Insider reported.
