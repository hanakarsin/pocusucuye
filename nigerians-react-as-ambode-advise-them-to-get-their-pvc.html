Nigerians React As Ambode Advise Them To Get Their PVC

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Monday, April 29, 2024

A former Governor of Lagos State, Akinwunmi Ambode has stirred reaction online after he advised youths to get Permanent Voters Card (PVC) ahead of the 2023 elections.

Ambode stated this in a statement he shared on his Twitter account on Tuesday.

He wrote, “After all we have been through, God has been faithful. Thank you for the overwhelming endorsement of our modest contributions to the growth of Lagos. It can only get better for Lagos, and indeed Nigeria.

“The youths are coming. The power is in their hands. Go and get your Permanent Voters Card (PVC) ready. Your future is NOW!”

Reacting, some Nigerians asked the former Governor if he is with the normal selection institute in the state or with the people of Lagos State.

Others asked if he would be contesting in the 2023 general elections.
