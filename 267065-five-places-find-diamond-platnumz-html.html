Five places you can find Diamond Platnumz

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Thursday, April 18, 2024

Diamond Platnumz, real name Naseeb Abdul Juma is a decorated Tanzanian Bongo Flava artist and dancer. To date, Diamond has done multiple hit tracks that have put the singer on the global map. Diamond Platnumz songs are adored by many as they combine different musical elements including R &B, Hip-hop, dance-hall, Afro-beat, and African musical heritage like taarab and dance.

Diamond Platnumz Music life and Growth

Naseeb interest in music began at a tender age when he was only in class five. His Mother Sanura Kassim popularly known as ‘Sandra’ nurtured her son’s interest by purchasing several albums of various musicians. Moreover, Sandra also assisted her son with the writing of songs and enrolled him to talent competitions with the hopes that one day Diamond will make it in music.

Diamond platinum Wealth

There are several ways through which Diamond Platinumz makes money in his career. Firstly, the more significant percentage of this wealth is gained from his sold out concerts in East Africa and beyond the region. Unofficial reports claim that the Tanzanian celebrity charges approximately KSH 370,000 per show in Tanzania and charges not less than Ksh 2.3 million for performing outside his native country. According to a recent interview at ETV, the award-winning artist publicized that his net worth is $4 million, making him the wealthiest musician in East and Central Africa.

Secondly, he has been appointed as the brand ambassador for several companies like Coca-Cola, Reg Gold, Uber, Vodacom and DSTV.

Thirdly, Diamond makes a lot of money from mobile phone ringtones as his songs are the most downloaded in East Africa by mobile subscribers.

The other way in which Platinumz makes money is via his YouTube channel that at the time of writing has more than 949,000 subscribers. When Diamond releases a new music video, it normally garners more than a million views in less than 48 hours; hence, this brings in a lot of cash from the YouTube advertisements.
