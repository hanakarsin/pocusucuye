Multimedia University of Kenya fee structure 2019

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Sunday, May 26, 2024

Multimedia University is a world class university in Kenya located in Nairobi County. This university is a registered university that mainly offers information and computer technology courses and mass media education. The university has a very motivated motto of leader in innovative technology. It was first established in the 1948 as a central training school and later progressed to a university. We tell you more about the institution, courses offered and the Multimedia University of Kenya fee structure.

Multimedia University is currently headed by the vice chancellor Catherine Kimura. The university offers certificate, degree and also diploma courses. The academic years for this university are comprised of two semesters from January to April and from September to December. In between is a long holiday that allows students to rest as they await the second semester. The university is however growing and this might change with time. Multimedia is well known for its diversity in technology and actually it takes part in the ongoing Konza City project that will greatly widen the technology and businesses in Kenya come 2030.

Multimedia University courses

The university is made up of 5 faculties;

1. Faculty of Engineering and Technology

This faculty admits all students that are qualified in engineering. The main courses offered are telecommunication engineering, electrical engineering, software engineering, mechanical engineering and mechatronics engineering. All these courses meet all the requirements with the education system and once an individual is out of this university, he/she is in a position to perform very well in the corporate world. Engineering is basically a tough course because of the technical sector but multimedia university is well equipped with laboratories that boost the technical expertise of individuals. The university has a library that is well equipped with books that help increase knowledge apart from that which is learnt in class. The university also offers diploma in engineering.

2. Faculty of Computing and Information Technology

The faculty of computing and information has certificate, diploma and degree courses. Computer studies are becoming a requirement for the job market and multimedia offers quality computer studies. To enrol all you have to do is log in to the multimedia website or go t the university and apply for computer. They also offer Bachelor of Science in computer technology bachelor of science in computer and also Bachelor of Science in ICT. All students have access to computers and the lecturers are well qualified. The students who go to multimedia university are normally fully equipped and once they go to the outside world they are professionals.
