How to Get the Deadpool &amp; Wolverine Popcorn Bucket

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Sunday, April 7, 2024

AMC Is the Only Theater Selling These Exclusive 'Deadpool & Wolverine' Popcorn Buckets

Show your allegiance to your favorite of the two heroes by choosing which of the two designs you want.

By Sara Belcher

Updated July 23 2024, 4:36 p.m. ET

The new Deadpool & Wolverine movie releases on July 26 and fans will have a brief opportunity to own their own popcorn bucket inspired by the film. What a time to be alive.

As more people flock to theaters for film releases, there have been plenty of special edition popcorn buckets and movie theater merch for fans to collect, available only for a limited time aligning with a film's release.

But how do you get the popcorn bucket for Deadpool & Wolverine? Here's what you need to know.

The 'Deadpool & Wolverine' bucket is exclusively available at AMC.

Those looking to get their hands on the limited-editing bucket will need to book their tickets at AMC, as the movie theater chain is the only one offering it. Movie-goers can purchase their bucket starting July 25, just one day before Deadpool & Wolverine hits theaters. The Wolverine popcorn bucket retails for $29.99, which includes the cost of a large popcorn included with the purchase.

Superfans can also snag the limited-edition Headpool popcorn vessel to go with their Wolverine one. The Wolverine popcorn bucket, designed by Deadpool, features the clawed superhero's face, with the popcorn being held in his open mouth. The Deadpool bucket, which retails for $39.99, allows players to pluck kernels from the comic character's cranium instead of his mouth.

AMC also has a variety of other limited-edition Wolverine & Deadpool merchandise available to pick up when attending your viewing, including two different GRIPPR cups with mystery toppers, each retailing for $24.99.

Fans can show their favoritism between the two heroes by selecting the popcorn bucket of their preferred masked vigilante (or collect them all through multiple viewings). These products are only available for a limited time, so be sure to snag them before they're gone.

AMC also has exclusive drinks celebrating the release of 'Deadpool & Wolverine.'

For viewers of legal drinking age, there are also two themed drinks available to purchase at eligible AMC locations, themed to each of the two titular characters. Movie goers can order either the Do-Over, a sweet strawberry cocktail, or the Crossover, a tart orange cocktail. Both are made with Aviation American Gin, Ryan Reynolds's personal gin brand.

Those who aren't old enough to drink (or just prefer not to) also have another option available to them at AMC theaters. The Coca-Cola Chimichanga Chaser and the Fanta Pina-Claw-Da are two soda-based drinks that are debuting specifically for the Deadpool & Wolverine movie premiere will also be available at participating locations.

In addition to these custom drinks, AMC will also have a shareable combo including Nashville Hot gourmet popcorn, two large drinks, and two candy selections for those looking to split with a friend.

Lastly, a ceramic mug commemorating the movie's release will also be available, for those looking for something to use in their day-to-day. Though limited-edition popcorn buckets aren't particularly new, it seems no one loves Deadpool & Wolverine like AMC does.
