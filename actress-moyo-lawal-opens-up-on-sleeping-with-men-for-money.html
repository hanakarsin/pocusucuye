Actress Moyo Lawal Opens Up On Sleeping With Men For Money

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

Popular Nigerian actress, Moyo Lawal has denied the rumours of sleeping with men for money.

The actress and skincare therapist via her Instagram handle revealed that she has made too many sacrifices in her life and career to soil it with inappropriateness.

The actress also noted that she’s a decent girl on the inside and way better than some of the average girls.

She added that she’s not married and has the liberty to do whatever she pleases, urging people who tell lies against her to stick to the truths when writing anything about her.

In her own words, she wrote; “I made way too many sacrifices in my life, I have said no to too many things in my life to live indecently. The last thing you can use to drag me down is a false accusation.

“I can beat my chest anywhere. When it comes to being decent inside, I am top 10. Whatever it is I have done in this life, I have not been as bad as a regular girl. I am not married but I have the liberty to do anything. If you are going to write stuff or say things, try and stick to the truth.”
