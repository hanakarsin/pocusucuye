15 best Western movies of all time

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Monday, January 1, 0001

Western movies are a genre of film which is centred on mainly the lives and culture that was in the America Old West during the later 19th century. The centre of attraction in these movies is usually a cowboy armed with the renowned revolver or a rifle wearing cowboy boots, bandannas, and buckskins.

Movies in this list are gauged primarily on directing and writing brilliance, their individual popularity and also according to their ratings.

Best Western movies of all time

Here are the 15 best Western movies according to IMDb ratings.

IMDb rating: 8.9/10

Originally il Buono, il Brutto, il cattivo, this is a classic Sergio Leone movie which is the best and his last movie in his so-called “Dollars” trilogy. Written by Sergio Leone, Luciano Vincenzoni, Furio Scarpelli and Age, this film depicts a bounty hunter joining another team of untrusted bounty hunters against a third party in a hasty rush to unearth a gold fortune buried in a cemetery.
