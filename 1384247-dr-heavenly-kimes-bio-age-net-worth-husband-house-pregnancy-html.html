Dr Heavenly Kimes is an award-winning Atlanta-based dentist, businesswoman, author, inspirational speaker, realtor, and reality TV star. She became famous after she began featuring in Bravo's reality show Married To Medicine.

Dr. Kimes owns a dental practice in Metro Atlanta called Smiles by Dr. Heavenly, and she is the CEO of Heavenly Dental Associates, Inc. She also owns a brand of dental products called SmileChangers. She is not only successful in her career, but she is also happily married with children and three dogs.
