Saint Jhn biography: Age, height, net worth, album, tour, girlfriend

Saint Jhn is a Brooklyn-born singer, rapper, songwriter, and record producer. He has written songs for some renowned singers such as Usher, Jidenna, Kiesza, and Hoodie Allen. He is also the founding member of the Godd Complexx music collection.

The rapper was featured in Beyonce's hit song Brown Skin Girl, and Beyonce's soundtrack album dubbed The Lion King: The Gift in 2019. Currently, Saint is managed by Kareem Biggs Burke. He also has a partnership with Los Angeles Reid's Hitco Records.
