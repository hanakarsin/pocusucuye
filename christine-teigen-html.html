Chrissy Teigen - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, August 1, 2024

American model who debuted in the 2010 Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue. In 2014, she was featured on the cover of the swimsuit issue.

Before Fame

She had one of her first modeling roles as an alternate model on Deal or No Deal.

Trivia

She hosted the 2015 Billboard Music Awards, and has appeared as a color commentator on the show Lip Sync Battle. 

Family Life

In 2013, she married musician John Legend. Their daughter, Luna Simone, was born in April of 2016 and they welcomed their second child, Miles Theodore, in May of 2018. She suffered complications while pregnant with a child named Jack, in 2020, and the child passed away before birth. In January 2023, she and John welcomed a second daughter named Esti. They also welcomed a son named Wren via surrogate in June 2023. 

Associated With

She portrayed herself working as a couples counselor in an episode of Inside Amy Schumer.
