Election 2015: Showbiz Interactive With President Jonathan

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Wednesday, March 6, 2024

It's less than two weeks before Nigerians go to the polls to elect those that would lead the country in the next four years.

At the presidential level, the two major candidates who have been adjudged by political analysts as favourites for the presidency are the incumbent President Goodluck Jonathan of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), and a former Head of State, Gen. Muhammadu Buhari, of the opposition All Progressives Congress (APC).

Both candidates have been holding political campaigns/rallies across the country in a bid to woo undecided voters to cast their votes in their favour.

On Sunday, March 15, 2015, in continuation of his interaction with Nigerians, President Jonathan had an interactive session with Nigerian celebrities at the prestigious Eko Hotel & Suites located on Lagos Island.

The event was attended by many stars of the Nigeria's entertainment industry.

Legit.ng brings you the key and exclusive updates from the event:
