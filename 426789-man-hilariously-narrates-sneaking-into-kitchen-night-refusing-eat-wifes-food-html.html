Man Hilariously Narrates Sneaking into the Kitchen at Night after Refusing to Eat Wife's Food

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Thursday, March 28, 2024
  • A Nigerian man, Dr Ayeni, said he could not hold a grudge against his wife for long after he had refused to eat
  • Hungry, the man snuck into the kitchen with the plan to eat the food without letting his partner know
  • After numerous attempts, he threw caution to the wind and feasted on the food, saying he could not die of hunger

A Nigerian doctor in the UK, Dr Ayeni, has narrated he refused to eat his wife's food because he was angry.

Hunger overcomes anger

Ayeni revealed that after his wife had gone to bed, he quietly went into the kitchen, picked a spoon, and started chipping away at the rice in the pot.

He did that so that his wife would not notice he later went to eat her food. The man stated that after he had scrapped at the food for a while, he went and got a bigger spoon.
