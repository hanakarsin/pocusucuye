Ryan O'Neal (TV Actor) - Trivia, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Friday, March 22, 2024

Starred with his daughter, Tatum O'Neal, in the popular show Paper Moon. He has played the recurring character Max Keenan in the TV show Bones. He has amassed over 60 on-screen roles throughout his acting career. 

Before Fame

In high school, he trained to become a Golden Glove boxer.

Trivia

He was in soap opera Peyton Place and Stanley Kubrick's Barry Lyndon. He played Ryan in Terrence Malick's 2015 film Knight of Cups. 

Family Life

He had a son with then-girlfriend Farrah Fawcett in 1985.

Associated With

He starred with Emily Deschanel in Bones.
