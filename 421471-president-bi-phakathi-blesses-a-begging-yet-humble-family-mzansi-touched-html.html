Internet Users Impressed after Philanthropist BI Phakathi Blesses Begging Yet Humble Family

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Monday, January 1, 0001
  • South African philanthropist BI Phakathi is continuing with his generous deeds after blessing a poor family from Zimbabwe in Venda
  • The Good Samaritan is a hit on social media once again for his kind deeds as after giving a poor family money
  • Social media users as far as Zimbabwe were impressed by the good deed, and some even promised to call the faceless man their president

South African faceless Good Samaritan BI Phakathi recently met a low-income family in Venda and blessed them with R2600 as they were roaming around the streets begging for clothes and food.

The woman said they are from Zimbabwe but found themselves in Mzansi looking for a better life, yet they are still struggling to make ends meet.

Phakathi gave the lady R1400 (KSh 10,515) before blessing the blind husband with R600 (KSh 4,506) and giving the toddler a handsome R600 (KSh 4,506) in cash for nappies and food.

BI Phakathi blesses humble woman with KSh 12,393

In a similar post, the generous philanthropist BI Phakathi is a man on a mission to bring smiles to many people who find it hard to make ends meet.

This time he brought tears to one woman who is a street vendor.

The Durban-based guy is seen in one of his video clips looking to buy from the lady and pays R100 KSh (751) for a beanie, but they continued to have a conversation.

The emotional lady said she makes R400 (KSh 3,007) or R800 (KSh 6,008) sometimes, but Phakathi surprised her with R1 650 (KSh 12,393).
