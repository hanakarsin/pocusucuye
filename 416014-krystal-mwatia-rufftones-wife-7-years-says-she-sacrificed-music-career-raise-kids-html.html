Krystal Mwatia: Rufftone's Wife of 7 Years Says She Sacrificed Music Career to Raise Kids

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Tuesday, August 6, 2024
  • Rufftone's wife Krystal Mwatia sacrificed her career to raise her children and give them the foundation they needed
  • Krystal said when she got married, she dedicated her time to serve her family because she wanted to personally raise her children
  • The celebrity couple has been married for seven years and their marriage keeps flourishing because, she says, they have an assignment bigger than the two of them

Susan Njoki popularly known as Krystal is a gospel singer and has been married to the popular artiste Rufftone for seven years now.

She exclusively talked to TUKO.co.ke about what she has been up to and life experiences as a celebrity's wife.

Krystal, a second born in a family of five, started her music journey back in 2006. She had just cleared high school and was about to join the university.

She has been in the gospel industry and earned herself a spot as one of the consistent female singers in the ministry.

However, at one point, the mother of two took a break from the limelight to focus on her first ministry; the family. This was after meeting her now husband Rufftone.

" I met Rufftone in 2005 while I was looking for a recording studio. What I liked about him that attracted me to him was that he was first, God fearing and that was important for me. I loved the fact that he was serving in Sunday school, so I was happy for the children. He was a great leader,'' she said.

The two fell in love and later held a colourful wedding at the Kenya Assemblies of God church in Buru Buru.

Having recorded great songs and built a successful career in the music industry, she took a break after starting a family.

"I was first managing Lampstand records which slowed me down. Then in 2014, I got married and God blessed us with many children, so that was the ministry I was serving in, raising God's generals until recently. I chose to sacrifice my career for them because I felt I needed to mould their foundation," she explained.

With the two being in the same industry, Krystal elucidated that she has never felt overshadowed by her husband's successful music career.

"When I met him, he was so famous and I was just starting, his style of music being so different from mine. Those two aspects have made it so easy for me. God called me as me. I don't feel overshadowed because we are both serving God," she added.

Being married for seven years and still going strong, the beautiful celebrity attributes submission to being the recipe that makes marriages even stronger.

She said that if God allowed her and the Mwikulu hitmaker to get married, then He had a reason and an assignment for them aside from raising children to serve Him in their generation.

"This gives us strength to keep going,'' she said.

As for people who desire to get married, Krystal believes marriages work.

"Always ask God to direct you to the person He has chosen for you. Invest in prayer, submission is key for the wife and only God can help you submit. Forgive and seek the will of God concerning your marriage," she said.

Krystal recently released a song dubbed Usiniache and featured her hubby and Asana. She is currently working on releasing a new single which will be followed up by an album in August.
