Political commentator Barrack Muluka has claimed that the government of Kenya collapsed a long time ago.

The government of Kenya is died

According to Muluka, the institution of government died a long time ago and the country has been on autopilot for the longest time ever.

He said there are no longer Cabinet meetings or resolutions that have come out of the Cabinet meetings.

" We have been on autopilot, that is why we no longer hear any Cabinet meetings of any resolutions that have come out of those meetings," Muluck said.

The political commentator argued the Cabinet secretaries used to meet once per week, contrary to nowadays.

Ruto working illegally

Maluku also took a swipe at Deputy President William Ruto saying he has been in the government for nine years, yet he has done nothing.

"You find a deputy president who is saying he has been doing this for nine years, working outside the regulations and the protocols. It means that for nine years he has been working illegally."

At the same time, he questioned the association of the DP with the Turkish businessman and what they were going to do in Uganda.
