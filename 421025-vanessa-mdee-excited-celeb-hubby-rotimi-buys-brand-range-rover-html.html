Vanessa's new gift came barely months after TUKO.co.ke reported that Rotimi flaunted his new mansion in Nigeria.

Rotimi buys family home in Nigeria

TUKO.co.ke understands Rotimi purchased a penthouse in Ikoyi, the most affluent neighbourhood of Lagos, Nigeria.

The Power star broke the news through social media where he revealed that he paid for the house in cash as a birthday present. to himself.

“As a bday gift to myself. I bought a penthouse in one of the most prime locations in Lagos with no mortgage (Ikoyi) full-on paid for. It’s important we know the value of real estate and legacy money. #InvestInYourSelf,” Rotimi tweeted.

With the purchase, he joined an exclusive list of celebrities with luxurious homes in the West African nation.
