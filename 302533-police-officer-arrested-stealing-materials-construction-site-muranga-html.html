Police officer arrested for stealing materials from construction site in Murang'a

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Friday, March 15, 2024

An Administration Police officer and three other civilians are set to appear in court on Monday, April 8, in connection to alleged theft of building materials at a construction site in Murang'a county.

The four suspects are said to have stolen the items worth KSh 3 million from a Chinese firm constructing the Northern Water Collector Tunnel in Kigumo Sub-county on three separate dates.

County police commander Josephat Kinyua confirmed the arrest were made on Saturday, April 6, after tracking down some suspects who had escaped to Nairobi.

The suspects then confessed they had been working in cahoots with the detained officer.

During the operation, police recovered most items believed to have been acquired illegally from the water tunnel site

“We will not let any officer hide under the National Police Service to commit crime, anyone connected to crime will be arrested and held liable for his or her own,” Kinyua said.

The project entails construction of a fully lined 11.8km, 3.2m diameter tunnel to collect 40% of water from Maragua, Gikige and Irati rivers and it is hoped it will increase water supply to Nairobi by 140 million liters per day.
