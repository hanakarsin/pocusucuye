Inspekta Mwala's actor Davis Mwambili fined KSh 30k after accident in which one died

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Davis Mwabili, popularly known as Inspekta Mwala, was on Thursday, September 19, found guilty of causing death by dangerous driving by a Mariakani court.

Mariakani senior principal magistrate Nelly C. Adalo sentenced the comedian to one year in jail or pay KSh 30,000 fine.

Mwabili, who features in popular TV drama series, Inspekta Mwala, however, paid the KSh 30, 000 fine to secure his freedom, K24 reported.

“As I am speaking to you now, I am in the SGR train coming to Nairobi after I attended court earlier today and paid the fine as ordered by the magistrate,” he was quoted.

The court heard that Mwabili, who would have served a one-year jail term had he not paid the fine, hit and killed a middle-aged man in Mazeras, Kilifi county, on August 26, 2019.

Mwabili had attempted to overtake another vehicle, but lost control of his and veered off the road, resulting in him hitting and killing one Ismael Mangi.

A postmortem examination conducted on Mangi at the Coast General Hospital morgue indicated that he succumbed to severe head injuries sustained in the crash.

Before his sentencing, the comedian in his mitigation, apologised to Ismael Mangi's family for leading to the demise of their son in the accident, albeit halfheartedly.

“I am very sorry. I also ask for forgiveness from the family of the deceased. I had not planned to cause the accident. I had no intention to kill, save that an accident occurred and I cannot have left where I was to go and cause the accident,” said Mwala

Mangi’s family, however, said it would go back to court to have the actor compelled to compensate for their kin’s death.

At the same time, Kenyans were left livid following the ruling which they argued was very lenient considering a life was lost.
