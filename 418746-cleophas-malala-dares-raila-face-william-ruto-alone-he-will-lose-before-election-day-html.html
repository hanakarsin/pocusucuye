Cleophas Malala Dares Raila to Face William Ruto Alone: &quot;He Will Lose Before Election Day&quot;

  • Malala reiterated that the western region, Central, coastal region and even some parts of Nyanza has pulled out of the ODM party
  • He said there is a need to have political friends and partners adding that is why they formed the One Kenya Alliance (OKA)

Kakamega senator Cleophas Malala has said that ODM party leader Raila Odinga has no national support at the moment.

Raila has no support

According to Malala, Raila is no longer supported in the country like before, saying the numbers ODM party is having was a collective effort from the National Super Alliance ( NASA) coalition.

In an interview with Citizen TV, the senator cited that the western, central, coast and even some parts of Nyanza region had pulled out of ODM.

The lawmaker noted if Raila faced Deputy President William Ruto alone, he will not get even one million votes, urging that his NASA partners were the ones supplying him with votes in the past elections.

"I dare ODM to take Raila Odinga to the ballot alone; he will lose before election day," he said.

The lawmaker said there is a need to have political friends and partners; that is why they formed the One Kenya alliance, saying it is a game-changer.

"We are the people who made sure that Raila came out with numbers in the previous elections, without having political friends and political partners there is nowhere Raila is going," Malala added.

At the time, he said NASA was dead and will never be revived, blaming NASA co-principals for dishonesty among themselves.
