'Why You Stubborn Like This'

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Thursday, March 7, 2024

Popular Nollywood actress  Nkechi Blessing has reacted after her colleague boldly disobeyed her first wife Funsho Adeoti.

Recall that Mercy Aigbe who stepped out in style for her colleague, Ini Edo’s 40th birthday party had shared some pictures and video of herself in Kazim Adeoti’s house.

Reacting to the photos, Funsho demanded that she take it down and gave the actress and her husband 12 hours to do the needful.

However, Mercy, calling her co-wife bluff, once again took to Instagram to post another video in her husbands house.

In the video the actress is seen on the staircase promoting her new movie.

Reacting to this, Nkechi Blessing questioned why Mercy Aigbe was stubborn since her name isn’t Nkechi.

“And your name is not Nkechi ooo, why you come stubborn like this?”. She wrote on her comment section.

Mercy Aigbe replied, “Nkechi please I am advertising my show oh!”.

Meanwhile, controversial Nollywood actor, Uche Maduagwu has dragged his colleague Yul Edochie over an old video of a happy moment shared online.

Yul Edochie got social media agog after sharing a lovey-dovey video with his first wife May and many people thought he had finally settled with her.
