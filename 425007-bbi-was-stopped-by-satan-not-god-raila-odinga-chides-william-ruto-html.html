BBI Was Stopped by Satan, Not God, Raila Odinga Chides William Ruto

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Thursday, March 28, 2024
  • Raila Odinga said BBI was good a document that meant well for all Kenyans
  • He argued counties were going to get more resources for infrastructure development and economic growth
  • Raila's sentiment came barely weeks after Deputy President William Ruto attributed the downfall of the Bill to God's intervention

Orange Democratic Party leader Raila Odinga has slammed those purporting that God stopped the Building Bridges Initiative (BBI) bill because it was a bad document.

Satan Stopped the BBI bill

According to Raila, the bill was stopped by Satan, who did not want the people of Kenya to get equal distribution of resources as proposed in the bill.

"I have heard some people saying that BBI was stopped by God, which is not true. Satan is the one who stooped the report because it was a good document, and satan is the one who always don't want good things to happen to people, " Raila said.
