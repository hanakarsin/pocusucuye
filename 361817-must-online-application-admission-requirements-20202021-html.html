MUST online application and admission requirements 2020/2021

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

University degrees are one of the most important pieces of accreditation that you can have. You need to have a university degree in order to get hired into some types of jobs. It is with this reason that Mbeya University of Science and Technology (MUST) offers you the chance to make your dreams come true through the MUST online application and admission program for the year 2020/2021.

Mbeya University of Science and Education, Formerly known as Mbeya Institute of Science and Technology is a public higher institution in Tanzania. It is located in Mbeya, southern Tanzania, and has another campus in Rukwa. The university has expanded over the years to provide various undergraduate, postgraduate and diploma programs to both Tanzanian and non-Tanzanian residents. The university boasts of a population of more than 5000 students. It is committed to providing the best university education in Science and Technology in the region, and it is ranked as one of the best 5 universities in the country.

Mbeya University of Science and Technology admission requirements

The entry requirements for successful admission into the degree and non-degree courses vary depending on the program you wish to enroll in. For certificate and diploma courses, you need to possess a Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) and a pass in at least four (4) non-religious subjects. Two or three of these subjects must be either mathematics, chemistry, biology, physics, or English.

If you wish to enroll in a degree course, you need at least two principal passes of at least "D" grade at the advanced level and a CSEE certificate with a minimum pass of "D" grade in at least four non-religious subjects. Holders of Diploma NTA Level 6 in the various diploma courses are eligible for the degree courses. For more details on the requirements for each course, download the admission requirements form on the MUST portal.

Mbeya University online application

MUST online application for undergraduate, bachelor's degree, diploma, certificate, and postgraduate students for 2020/2021 academic year is currently open. Here is a step by step joining instruction for Mbeya University of Science and Technology:

  • Visit the MUST Online Application System (MUST-OAS)
  • Choose the MUST online application of your choice either "MUST Main Campus Online Application" or "MUST Rukwa Campus Online Application".
  • Once you click on the link, you will be redirected to another page.
  • If you are new on the platform, you should click on the link "Start Application".
  • Enter all the details in the section provided, including the application type, where you completed your O'level, your Form IV index number. The Form IV index Number must be the same as in Form IV Certificate
  • Tap on the "Next Step" button to proceed to the next step. You must read admission requirements before selecting the program of choice.
  • Follow the prompts until you get to the payment section where the system will generate the Control Number.
  • You are required to pay a non-refundable application fee of 10,000/= through Tigo Pesa, M-Pesa, Airtel Money, or through National Microfinance Bank (NMB) OR Tanzania Postal Bank (TPB PLC) after obtaining Control Number from Online Application System(OAS).
  • In case you experience any technical problems during the application process, please contact the IT Staff using the numbers 0719935990, 0784623637, 0763007740. For issues to deal with courses of study and clarifications of entry requirements, you should contact the Admissions Office using the phone numbers 0767349139, 0688226116, 0719884251

Application deadline for the 2020/2021 intake

The deadline for the application of diploma and postgraduate courses is October 30, 2020. Once the admission portal is closed, new applications will not be submitted. If you wish to apply for admission during the 2020/2021 academic year, submit your application early enough to avoid being left out. The names of successful applicants will be published through the Government newspaper and the university website.

Fee structure and other expenses requirements

If you are pursuing an Ordinary Diploma (NTA Level 4-6) program, you can join the university under either Government sponsorship or private sponsorship. Students pursuing Bachelor's Degree and Postgraduate programmes are encouraged to apply for scholarships or loan from the Higher Education Students Loan Board (HESLB).

You can access the fee structure from the MUST website by clicking on the appropriate link, either Ordinary Diploma Applicants, Bachelor Degree Applicants, or Postgraduate Applicants.
