Korede Bello Returns To School

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Saturday, April 20, 2024

Mavin Records artiste Korede Bello has returned back to school.

Korede Bello had his Matriculation today  at the Nigeria Institute of Journalist (NIJ) which held at NIJ Premise in Ogba to study a Higher National Diploma in mass communication.

[article_adwert]

Revcently Korede won ‘The Song of the Year’ award at the 4th Annual Golden Icons Academy Movie Awards (GIAMA) that held last night in Houston, Texas. The event was hosted by Nigerian comedian and actor AY Makun.

Korede Bello released his follow up single, ‘Somebody Great’ about a week ago and the song has since gathered massive air play and acceptance for the messages inherent in it.

On 28 February 2014, Korede got signed to Mavin Records under which he has recorded popular songs like “African Princess” and “Godwin”. He is currently working on his first studio album and cites Asa and 2face Idibia as people he draws inspiration from.

Good luck to him.
