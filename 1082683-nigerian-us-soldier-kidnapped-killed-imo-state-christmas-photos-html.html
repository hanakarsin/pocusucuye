Nigerian US soldier kidnapped and killed after he came back to Imo state for Christmas (photos)

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Monday, August 12, 2024

A Nigerian US soldier has been kidnapped and killed after he came to his hometown in Imo state to see his family and celebrate Christmas.

According to the Facebook user, Lilian Ndiukwu, who shared the tragic story, her elder brother who is a veteran US soldier came home to celebrate Christmas in Imo state but he was abducted and killed.
