Alexis Fawx - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Friday, August 2, 2024

Media personality, entrepreneur, and adult film actress. She is the host of the live comedy show and podcast known as The High as Fawx Show. She is also the owner of High as Fawx Coffee and in 2022, she collaborated with Astronomy Aleworks to create her own special-edition stout beer called MILF Stout. She has amassed over 2.5 million followers on her alexisfawxlive Instagram account.  

Before Fame

She appeared in her first adult film in 2010 and earned a contract with BangBros shortly thereafter. After returning from a hiatus eight years later, she briefly worked for the production company Hard X before becoming a BRAZZERS exclusive actress.

Trivia

Her performances in adult films have been nominated for multiple awards. In 2020 and 2022, she won MILF/Cougar Artist of the Year from the AVN Awards.

Family Life

She served in the military for nearly four years after high school but was forced to return home early in order to take care of her family after her father passed away.

Associated With

In March 2023, she provided an exclusive interview for adult film photographer Holly Randall on her web series Holly Randall Unfiltered.
